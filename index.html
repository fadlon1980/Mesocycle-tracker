Mesocycle Plan: May 6 – June 14

Week 1: May 6–10

Baseline, technique, find working weights. RPE 6–7.

Week 2: May 11–17

Add reps or small load increases. RPE 7.

Week 3: May 18–24

Hypertrophy accumulation. Main lifts RPE 7–8, accessories RPE 8.

Week 4: May 25–31

Peak productive volume. Add one extra set to 2–3 priority exercises.

Week 5: June 1–7

Final overload week. Accessories may reach RPE 8–9; main lifts stay around RPE 8.

Week 6: June 8–14

Deload. Reduce total sets by 40–50%, use lighter loads, RPE 6–7.
